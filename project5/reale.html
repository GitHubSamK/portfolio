Dr. Samuel Reale Room

$199 $229

The Dr. Samuel Reale Room is located on the ground floor of the Main House and was once used as a parlor for the Franklin family and their guests. This room has an alcove with windows on three sides, the perfect spot to sit in the two comfortable wing chairs. With its high queen-size four-poster bed and coffered ceiling, the unique decor of this room is cozy and warm, yet elegant. The private bathroom includes a shower
